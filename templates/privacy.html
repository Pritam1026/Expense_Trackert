Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic interaction data such as pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the experience.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To identify bugs and improve the reliability of the application.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Expense data you enter is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not currently integrate with or share data with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly and you will be notified before any such integration takes effect.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app or contact the project maintainer directly. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
